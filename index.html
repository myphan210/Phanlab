About Us

Our lab combines wet-lab experiments and dry-lab computational analyses on a range of samples to investigate virus emergence, transmission and evolution. In the lab, we perform sample processing and deep sequencing using Oxford Nanopore technology. We process the resulting sequencing data from base-calling, quality control, read assemblies, read/contigs identification and phylogenetic analyses of detected virus pathogens to investigate virus transmission and evolution - In this process, we develop our custom bioinformatics tools and scripts to help with the analyses. In addition, we study the virus patterns identified from a variety of sample types and sources to aid public health disease control and management and help answer important human health questions.

We welcome everyone who share similar interests to join our lab. Whether you are undergraduate or graduate (masters and PhD) students or postdocs, please feel free to email me (My.VT.Phan@asu.edu) to explore possibilities to join our team.
